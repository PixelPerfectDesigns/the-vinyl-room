Our Story

More Than
a Bar.

The Vinyl Room started with a simple idea: bring people together through music, food, and good vibes. What began in Wappingers Falls in 2017 has grown into Beacon's most singular night out — a two-floor destination where the cocktails are craft, the records are real, and everyone is welcome.

"We're about positivity — a place where everyone feels welcome. Leave it at the door." — TVR Team

What We Are

Four Experiences.
One Address.

Craft Cocktails

Meticulously built drinks from a bar that takes the pour seriously. Rotating seasonal list, craft beer, and a wine selection that surprises.

Culinary Tapas

Chef-driven small plates built for sharing. Korean nachos, beef tallow fries, crispy wings — food that earns its own reputation.

Record Shop

Browse, buy, and sell vinyl while you drink. An in-house collection spanning genres and decades — no two visits sound the same.

Lounge + Arcade

Head downstairs. A basement lounge with vintage arcade games open Friday and Saturday nights. Vinyl only on the decks — no laptops.
