About the Shop

No Two Visits
Sound the Same.

The record shop lives inside the bar — because that's how it should be. Pull a sleeve off the shelf, order a cocktail, and stay a while. The collection spans jazz, soul, rock, hip-hop, reggae, Latin, funk, blues, and the psychedelic and strange.

No collection is too large or too small. If you've got records sitting idle, we buy. Smaller collections? Walk in. Larger? We'll come to you.

Selling Your Collection

We Buy.
You Get Cash.

01

Smaller Collection?

Just bring them in. Walk up with your collection, we'll take a look on the spot and make you an offer in cash. No appointment needed.

02

Got More? We'll Come to You.

We're located in Beacon and will travel within a reasonable distance to view larger collections. Reach out and we'll set it up.

03

Fair Price, Cash on the Spot

We believe in paying fair prices. No consignment, no waiting — you walk out with cash in hand.

04

What We Don't Buy

We're not purchasing classical, show tunes, big band, country, or anything pre-1960 generally. Not sure? Use the form below or give us a call.
