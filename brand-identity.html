Interactive Identity
Signature Moments
Three interactions most users have never seen before — each brand-specific, each fully functional, each built to be remembered.
01
Navigation
The Needle Drop
The mobile menu trigger is a vinyl record SVG. Tap it — the record spins 720° and a full-screen overlay opens. A coral scan line drops top-to-bottom, like a stylus hitting a groove, before each nav link stagger-reveals in sequence. It's a hamburger menu that became the most memorable thing on the site.
02
Atmosphere
The Groove Texture
The hero background is a CSS-only concentric record groove — pure repeating-radial-gradient, no images, no JS. Centered off-axis at 68% 50%, it creates depth and motion without loading a single asset. Imperceptible until you look for it. Unforgettable once you do.
03
Reveal
Ghost Watermark Text
Inner page heroes use giant -webkit-text-stroke outlined text as a background layer — "EVENTS" at 320px, visible only as a coral outline at the bottom of the viewport. Poster design DNA applied to web. The word is decoration and wayfinding at the same time. Something you photograph.
